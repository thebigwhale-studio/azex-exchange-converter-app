Privacy Policy — AZEX Exchange Converter

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

AZEX Exchange Converter ("the App", "we", "us") is a mobile application for Android and iOS that provides real-time cryptocurrency and fiat currency conversion. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. It applies to the App as distributed on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect information that directly identifies you (such as your name or email). However, like most free apps, the App uses third-party advertising and analytics services that collect certain device and usage information as described below.

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or request:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Account credentials or passwords (the App has no login)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Your contacts, photos, or files
  • Precise GPS location

Information Stored On Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your selected currency pairs and conversion preferences
  • Display and interface settings

This data stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data.

Information Collected Automatically

To keep the App free, we use third-party advertising and analytics services. These services may automatically collect limited technical and usage information, including:

  • A mobile advertising identifier (e.g., Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA, where permitted)
  • Device information such as device model, operating system version, and language
  • General (approximate) location inferred from IP address, used for ad relevance
  • App usage and interaction data, and crash/diagnostic reports

This information is collected in a form that is not intended to personally identify you, and is used to display advertisements, measure app performance, and diagnose crashes.

Currency Rate Data

To provide up-to-date exchange rates, the App fetches publicly available currency and cryptocurrency rate data from third-party rate providers. These requests contain only what is needed to retrieve rates (such as currency codes) and do not include personal information added by the App. The rate provider may log standard technical data (such as IP address) as part of normal traffic handling.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where you have not opted out. You can control ad personalization in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads (reset or opt out of Ads Personalization)
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising

For more on how Google uses this data, see the Google Privacy & Terms.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App uses analytics and crash-reporting services to understand aggregate usage and improve stability. These services collect anonymous or pseudonymous usage and diagnostic data and do not identify you personally.

Third-Party Services

The third-party services integrated in the App operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob / Google Analytics — policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided such information, please contact us and we will address it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data and to opt out of personalized advertising. Because the App does not collect information that identifies you directly, you can exercise most controls through your device's advertising and privacy settings, or by contacting us at the address below.

Data Retention

Preferences stored on your device remain until you delete them or uninstall the App. Data collected by advertising and analytics providers is retained according to their respective policies.
