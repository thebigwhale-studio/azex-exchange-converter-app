Privacy Policy — AZEX Exchange Converter

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

AZEX Exchange Converter ("the Extension") is a browser extension that provides real-time cryptocurrency and fiat currency conversion. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension does and does not collect, and how any data is handled.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The Extension is designed to work without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

Information Stored Locally

The Extension may store the following data locally on your device only, using the browser's built-in storage (chrome.storage):

  • Your selected currency pairs and conversion preferences
  • Display and interface settings

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to any server owned or operated by us, and is not accessible to any third party. You can remove this data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's extension data.

Network Requests

To provide up-to-date exchange rates, the Extension fetches publicly available currency and cryptocurrency rate data from third-party exchange rate API providers. These requests:

  • Contain only the information necessary to retrieve exchange rates (e.g., currency codes)
  • Do not include any personal information or unique user identifiers added by the Extension
  • Are made directly from your browser to the rate provider

Please note that third-party API providers may log standard technical information (such as IP addresses) as part of normal web traffic handling. Their handling of such data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Permissions

The Extension requests only the minimum browser permissions required for its core functionality:

  • Storage — to save your preferences locally on your device
  • Network access to rate provider APIs — to retrieve current exchange rates

The Extension does not request permissions to read or modify your browsing data on websites you visit.

Analytics and Tracking

The Extension does not use:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks or trackers
  • Cookies or fingerprinting techniques
  • Any form of user behavior monitoring

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer any user data to third parties, because we do not collect any user data in the first place.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is a utility tool that operates without data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Compliance

This Extension complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies and the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
